Mission Statement

At Champ Kidz our main objective is to provide holistic and complete growth of our little students and to give them the best pre-school experience with state of the art facilities and infrastructure.

We wish to provide the right boost to the kids at this tender age to kick start their journey in becoming “Champ Kidz”!

In this endeavour we wish to work on their abilities and push them to grow their character and skills along with giving them the confidence to becoming bright young minds. We will follow a balanced mix of orthodox Montessori teaching techniques along with some unique teaching styles and also follow some International Montessori methodologies so as to give our young ones a thorough preparation in the pre-school arena.

“A bright beginning to a bright future”

The main objective of Champ Kidz is to give our young students a bright start which is very crucial in achieving greater results in the future. We believe that a strong foundation with the right skills and hands-on experience will give our students the right preparation to take on the challenges of school. In this vigour we will strive to give our young minds all the knowledge they need in their journey with the best techniques, toys and learning equipments at their disposal.

This is a crucial stage for both parents and kids alike
with some very pressing questions.

“What is the purpose of pre-school education?”

The answer to this is that the very foundation of a child’s education lies on pre-school education. Whatever the child learns in the future is based on the skills and knowledge he or she acquires at this young age. Also if the right platform is set for learning the child finds it easy to cope with pressure and can learn and grasp at a faster rate.

Thus to lay this founding stone to a bright future ahead pre-school education is of utmost importance.

“What is the right age to start my child’s education?”

The below table gives the generally accepted years for the start of pre-school education

Group Age
Play Group 1.8 years to 2.5 years
Nursery 2.5 years to 3.5 years
Junior Kindergarten 3.5 years to 4.5 years
Senior Kindergarten 4.5 to 5.5 years

“How helpful is Montessori education?”

Montessori education is very crucial in a childs life as it sets the tone for the years to come. It helps prepare the kids to take the vigour of School head on. It is a proven fact that children with a pre-school education are able to fare far better than students who don’t. They develop key skills right before joining school and also acquaints them to other kids which results in a holistic social growth along with academic growth.
